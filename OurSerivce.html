Interior Design, Renovation & Remodeling

All of our designs begin with an in-depth meeting to discuss your project and your design needs. This is followed by an in-depth analysis of your space in order to maximize the light, the form and the function. We then draw up a proposal or scope of work encompassing budget, timetable and materials and finishes. Once formally approved, the project is launched and we will be on our way to creating a design that hits all of the right notes. Depending on the project, this could include:
  • Concept development
  • Technical drawings
  • Project tendering and/or management
  • Final Decor and accessory acquisition
No project is too large or too small. Come and see us for a consultation on your dream space today!
