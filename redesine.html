Who Are We?

Who Are We? Specializing in residential and commercial spaces, Lemon Lime offers clients a fresh and inspired approach to interior spaces. Lemon Lime Design, featuring Jone Wright, has earned top reviews and gained a fabulous reputation since its founding in 2006. We plan residential and commercial spaces that are refreshingly unique while still being personal and comfortable. Creating beautiful interiors which reflect your personal style is an art. At Lemon Lime, we take the time to plan spaces that reflect you and your personal style. Our unique perspective is grounded in both art and architecture, using colour, form and materials in creatively fresh ways to create uniquely inspired spaces. Our services include: Interior space planning, Renovation & Re-modelling, Custom Upholstery & Finishing; and a retail store in which to find that last finishing detail. Whether your project is large or small, we look forward to working together. There's much to see here. So, take your time, look around, and learn all there is to know about us. We hope you enjoy our site and take a moment to drop us a line.
